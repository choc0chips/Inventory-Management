# StockWise

## Register

product

## Users

Small business owners managing their own inventory. They need quick access to stock levels, product info, and movement history without wading through enterprise complexity. They're often multitasking, so every action should feel immediate.

## Product Purpose

StockWise tracks products, stock movements, suppliers, categories, and imports in a single clean dashboard. Success means the owner can open the app, understand their inventory health in seconds, and act (restock, adjust, export) without friction.

## Brand Personality

Modern, clean, efficient. The interface stays out of the way. Data is the hero. Tone is professional but not cold, confident but not flashy.

## Anti-references

Overstyled dashboards with heavy gradients, excessive glassmorphism, and decorative clutter. Avoid anything that looks like a Bootstrap admin template or legacy ERP.

## Design Principles

1. **Glanceable status**: Stock health, alerts, and KPIs must be readable at a glance. Color and position carry meaning.
2. **Frictionless actions**: Common workflows (add product, restock, adjust) take one click, never a hunt through menus.
3. **Data integrity visible**: Stock levels are the source of truth. Movement records should back every change with clear audit trails.
4. **Consistent patterns**: Tables, forms, modals, and badges follow the same conventions across products, categories, suppliers, and reports.
5. **Theme-aware by default**: Every surface works in light and dark mode. No hardcoded colors that break in one theme.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard best practices: sufficient contrast, focus indicators, semantic HTML, responsive layout. No specific WCAG level required, but aim for AA where reasonable.
